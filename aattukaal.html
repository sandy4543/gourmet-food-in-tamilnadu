Aatukaal Paya - Mutton paya recipe - A best sidedish for idiyappam, aapam, idly, dosa. This paya is prepared with 1 goat legs(4 ). Paya is a popular dish made with slight variation in most parts of Tamil Nadu. This recipe is made with the legs of the goat and hence its name Paya.

In Tamil Nadu, mutton paaya is usually made for breakfast on special days like Diwali or any family get-together etc. The unbeatable combination are aapam with paaya and idiyappam with paaya.

The same combination of dishes are served in many famous and authentic restaurants all over Tamil Nadu.



Mutton paya also known as Aatukal Paya has a very creamy gravy with an intense flavour that comes from cooking the legs of the goat.The legs are cooked long enough until it breaks sown and releases its flavour and essence into the paya. Aatukaal soup is also served for sick patients and new moms to regain their health.

Feet of goat cooked into aatukal paya is an authentic dish where legs cooked into thick soup with indian spices. Paya is a type of spiced trotters which is usually match with either rice or appam.
