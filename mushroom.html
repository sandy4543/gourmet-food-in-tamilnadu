Mushroom Biriyani

Mushroom biryani ( known as Kaalan Biryani in Tamil) is one of the delicacies of vegetarian cooking and this mushroom biryani recipe is one of my favorite recipes. Mushrooms are exotic ingredients of cooking, they have little flavor on their own but they will absorb the flavor of all the seasoning applied to them. Mushrooms are rich in nutrients and are a good source of anti oxidants while having lower calories

Mushroom biryani is also known as Kalan Biryani in Tamil Nadu as mushrooms are called ‘kalan’ in Tamil language.

Mushroom biryani is the classic vegetarian version of biryani with meaty mushrooms, tender fluffy rice and flavorful spices. I share two delectable mushroom biryani recipes from the South Indian cuisine. These recipes requires only one pot and are made in less than forty five minutes! A spicy, tasty mushroom and rice recipe, these vegetarian mushroom biryanis are perfect for a delicious weeknight meal.
