Vatha kuzhambu is one of the simplest recipe I make at times is vatha kulambu. This vathal kuzhambu recipe and method gives you a really tasty and tongue-tickling kuzhambu. The recipe is vegan and quite nutritious as well.

Duck gravy is great to add to idlis, dosa, rice. Today we will see how to make duck curry khao at home in Vellore style.



Clean the duck with turmeric powder.Add curry, peeled cloves and turmeric powder and soak for 1/2 hour in boiling water. Fry the vadagam in a pan with oil and keep it aside, fry the coconut in the same oil until golden brown. Grind North+Coconut core and finally add Cumin and grind it.Leave oil in a pan and add biryani leaves and saute onions, tomatoes, green chillies and peppers one after the other.

When it is fried, pour the curry with tamarind and boil it. When the curry is cooked, add the ground coconut paste and let it cool after 5 minutes .This group is better if it is sour.
